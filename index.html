Process Improvement

Six Sigma Green Belt — SSI Remap (Euroclear Netherlands)
1.24 FTE Saved 8–12% Fail Rate Reduction Six Sigma DMAIC
Identified that incorrect Standard Settlement Instructions (SSIs) for multiple ISINs were routing Euroclear Netherlands trades to Euroclear Brussels, creating a systemic failure requiring daily manual cross-bookings between two Nostro accounts. Conducted root cause analysis, proposed and validated the solution with the onshore team, and used the client's banking software to remap SSIs to the correct Nostro account — eliminating all manual intervention entirely and forming the core of the total 1.5+ FTE saved over 2 years.
Tax Reporting SLA Improvement
1-Day TAT Reduction Permanently Adopted
Identified that monthly Tax Return reports for French, Greek, and Italian markets were being prepared on the 1st day of the following month. Proposed and implemented completing the reports on the last working day of the reporting month instead — reducing turnaround time by one full business day. The change was validated, approved, and permanently adopted by the team.
Intellitracs Dual Case-Generation Bug Fix
System Integrity Restored IT Escalation & Fix
Detected a system error where cases were being swept twice in Intellitracs, causing duplicate case generation instead of clearing the underlying breaks. Documented the issue, escalated to the IT team with full reproduction steps, validated the root cause analysis, and confirmed the permanent fix — preventing ongoing data integrity issues across the reconciliation workflow.
Project Onboarding & Scope Expansion
+3 FTE Equivalent Scope Onshore–Offshore Migration
Led the virtual onshore-to-offshore migration of a new project workstream, effectively adding the equivalent of three full-time employees' worth of operational scope to the team without additional headcount — independently managing a complex transition across geographies.
